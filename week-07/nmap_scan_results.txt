QUESTIONS:
1. What Nmap flag did you use to find the specific software versions running on these ports?
Answer: I used the -sV flag, which enables Nmap’s version detection engine to interrogate 
open ports and identify the running service software and its specific version.

2. Why is it dangerous to run an intense scan (like -A) on a fragile legacy system?
Answer: Intense scans like -A can generate a large amount of traffic and send complex probes,
which may overwhelm or crash fragile legacy systems, causing service disruption or downtime.
